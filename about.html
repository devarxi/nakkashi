Our Craftsmanship

Born from Tradition

At Nakkashi, we believe in the beauty of handmade products. Each piece is carefully crafted by skilled artisans who pour their passion and expertise into every detail.

Founded in 2018 by Jay Ganesh Abhale, Nakkashi began as a small workshop with a vision to preserve traditional Indian crafting techniques while creating products that fit seamlessly into modern lifestyles.

Our mission is to support local artisans and provide them with a platform to showcase their skills to a global audience while ensuring fair wages and sustainable working conditions.
